## The Key Distinction

### Standard Model (Shell Model)
**Magic numbers at specific Z or N** (shell closures):
- Proton magic: Z = 2, 8, 20, 28, 50, 82, 126
- Neutron magic: N = 2, 8, 20, 28, 50, 82, 126, 184

**Prediction**: Enhanced stability for isotopes/isotones with magic Z or N
- Example: Z=50 (Tin) has 10 stable isotopes
- Example: N=82 has 7 stable isotones

**Mechanism**: Quantum shell closures (like electron shells in atoms)

### QFD Model
**Magic numbers at specific A** (bulk mass resonances):
- Bulk mass magic: A = 50, 124, 130, 136, 138
- Spacing: ΔA ≈ 6 (geometric packing period)

**Prediction**: Enhanced stability for isobars (same A, different Z) at magic A
- Example: A=124 has 3 stable isobars (Z=50,52,54)
- Example: A=130 has 3 stable isobars (Z=52,54,56)

**Mechanism**: Geometric packing resonances in soliton structure
