**CORRECTED CONVENTIONS:**
- Distance modulus: μ = -2.5 log₁₀(F) + C
- Residual: Δμ = μ_obs - μ_model
- Δμ > 0: Data fainter (dimming)
- Δμ < 0: Data brighter (lensing)

- FDR (Flux-Dependent Redshift): τ_FDR = ξ · η' · √(F/F_ref)
