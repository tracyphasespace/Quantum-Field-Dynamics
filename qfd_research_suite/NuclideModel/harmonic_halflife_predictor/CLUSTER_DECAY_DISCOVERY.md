## Executive Summary

Cluster decay (e.g., Ra-223 → Pb-209 + C-14) is NOT random fragmentation.

It follows a **Pythagorean theorem in harmonic space**:

```
N²_parent ≈ N²_daughter + N²_cluster
```

Where N² represents **deformation energy** (harmonic resonance amplitude²).
